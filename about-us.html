About us Page

🐾Sad Reality

In a time where pets in shelters are increasing in numbers and too few people consider adoption, an excessive number of abandoned pets are in shelters all around the country. In Australia between 2018 and 2019, 33,863 dogs were received by the RSPCA, along with 51,170 cats, and 39,113 other animals. Of these, 4,308 dogs, 11,740 cats, and 19,541 other animals were euthanised (RSPCA 2019). The potential benefits of our application "Furever Friends" are the reduction of these numbers.

🐾Business Problem

Part of the problem is due to too many people who are uneducated about pet ownership and don’t consider the responsibilities that come with it. We are aiming to address both of these underlying problems, by implementing a system to provide people with a platform to search for, and adopt their pets of choice in a simple process, while attempting to filter out unsuitable pet owners.

🐾Our Solution

Our match-making system "Furever Friends" aims to help reduce euthanized pets by making the adoption process easier for people to adopt pets in shelters instead of buying them elsewhere. Our system also aims to reduce animal neglect by educating people who may be less aware of the responsibilities, so that they may seek to learn more about their pet’s welfare.
