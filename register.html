Screener Questions

Q1- Are you 18 or over?



Q2- Have you ever owned a pet before?



Q3- Are you planning on giving this pet as a gift to someone else?




Q4- Who will be mainly responsible for this pet?





Q5 - Who in your home is happy with the new pet adoption?





Q6 - If you are thinking of adopting a dog, what kind of space will they live in?





Q7 - What will be provided to the pet for stimulation when they are bored?




Q8 - How would the pet be taken care of if their owner/family were away from home for more than a day?





Q9 - Would this pet be cared for it's entire life?




Q10 - What would you feed the pet?




Q11 - Why do you want to adopt a pet?




Q12 - How would you decide how much money to spend on your pet when it comes to flea/worm treatment, special food or vet bills?




Q13 - Do any of your family members have any allergies that would be affected by adopting a pet?
